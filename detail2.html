Let's Go

V2 Logo

V2 is an upcoming successor to VATSIM (online network for virtual pilots). V2 is looking to create an environment for both the professional and the novice pilot; with the addition of fully certified air traffic controllers.

The V2 logo speaks for itself. A triangle with flowing cutouts to make 2 "v" letters. V2 is a technical term in the aviation industry indicating the minimum safe speed for one engine climbs on aircraft.
