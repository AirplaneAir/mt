Airstar's Logo

Airstar is an aerospace firm that works with aircraft designs and other aviation-related problems. Airstar approached me with little planning, simply a name and a concept.

The logo was an easy choice in my opinion. A geometric star with block italic lettering seemed to fit the industry standard. The colors I used were a deep blue and white (inverted in this logo); blue again fit the industry standard, but yet was able to stand apart.
